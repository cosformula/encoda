Module codecs/rpng

Reproducible PNGs (rPNGs)

This codec decodes from, and encodes to, a rPNG which embeds the JSON-LD of the node into the tEXt chunk of the PNG.

See http://www.libpng.org/pub/png/spec/1.2/PNG-Chunks.html#C.Anc-text
