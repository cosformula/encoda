Module codecs/xlsx

XLSX: Microsoft Excel

Also acts as base implementation for other spreadsheet-like formats including ods and csv. Uses js-xlsx`https://github.com/SheetJS/js-xlsx) to decode and dump spreadsheets and transforms to/from the it's (Common Spreadsheet Format)[https://github.com/SheetJS/js-xlsx#common-spreadsheet-format] and Stencila schema instances.
